Broken Telephone

Level 1 — Edit the Beat

Everyone starts from the same skeleton. Edit it — swap sounds, change notes, adjust rhythm — to make it your own. When your beat gets selected, the room listens and tries to edit their skeleton to match what they hear. Then compare.

Level 1 · edits only
The skeleton
open in strudel.cc or
paste into strudel.cc 
$: sound("bd ~ sd ~")
$: sound("hh*4").gain(0.4)
$: note("c3 ~ ~ ~").sound("piano")
what you can change
linewhat to try
drums swap bd/sd positions, add/move ~ rests, try cp or oh
hats change *4 to *2 or *6, adjust .gain(), swap hh for oh
melody change notes (c3 e3 g3 bb3), move ~ rests around, swap piano for another sound
rules
keep all 3 $: lines no adding new lines no effects (.lpf .delay .room) max *8 no .slow() or .fast()
notes you can use in the melody
// these 5 notes sound good together — pick any of them
c3  eb3  f3  g3  bb3

// or for a brighter mood, these 7
c3  d3  e3  f3  g3  a3  b3
after reveal — discuss
discussion prompts

What did the room get right — rhythm, pitch, or density?

Did anyone use different code to get the same sound?
