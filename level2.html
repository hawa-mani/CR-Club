Broken Telephone

Level 2 — Add to the Beat

The skeleton is a finished groove — kick, bass, and quiet hats already locked in. Your job is to add new $: lines on top. The original three lines stay exactly as they are. When your beat gets selected, everyone tries to hear what you added and recreate those additions on top of their own skeleton.

Level 2 · additive only

what to add — ideas
lead melody
$: note("c4 ~ eb4 ~ g4 ~ f4 ~")
  .sound("gm_lead_1_square")
  .gain(0.6)
counter melody
$: note("g4 ~ ~ ~ f4 ~ eb4 ~")
  .sound("gm_violin")
  .gain(0.5)
extra percussion
$: sound("~ ~ ~ cp ~ ~ ~ cp")
  .gain(0.6)
high melody
$: note("c5 ~ ~ ~ bb4 ~ ~ ~")
  .sound("triangle")
  .gain(0.4)

discussion prompts

What's the minimum addition that makes the biggest difference?
